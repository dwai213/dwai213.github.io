University of California Marching Band

I first held a trombone in 5th grade. Ever since then, I have continued to play it for my enjoyment. For the last three years of my undergrad, I have been a part of the Cal Band as a first trombonist. As a member of Cal Band, I have performed at AT&T Park, at Memorial Stadium (pre and post renovations), while skiing down at a Tahoe snow resort, on a moving cable car in San Francisco, and in the SF Giant's victory parade for their 2nd World Series Championship.

  • At the Giant's 2nd Victory Parade, the Cal Band was once again invited to participate in the celebration
